Conclusion

What I found

  • The effect of music depends on the task and the person.
  • Instrumental music at moderate volume often supported focus.
  • Lyrical music was more distracting during reading/writing tasks.

Limitations

  • Small sample size
  • Different music preferences/familiarity
  • Mood, sleep, stress, and distractions are hard to control

Improvements for next time

  • More participants and repeated trials
  • More controlled volume tracking
  • Separate data by task type (math vs reading vs memorization)

Recommendation

If you want a safe study choice: try instrumental music at low/moderate volume, and switch to silence for heavy reading/writing or memorization.
