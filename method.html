Method

Participants

Students/volunteers completed short study tasks under different music conditions. (Replace this with your real participant info: number, grade range, etc.)

Music Conditions

  • Silence (no music)
  • Instrumental (no lyrics)
  • Lyrical (songs with words)

Tasks

  • Reading comprehension
  • Writing / short response
  • Math practice
  • Memorization (terms/definitions)

Measurements

  • Accuracy: correct answers / score
  • Time: completion time
  • Self-rating: focus level (1–10) after each condition

Controls / Fair Testing

  • Same task difficulty for each condition
  • Similar time limits
  • Volume kept consistent (or recorded)
  • Same environment if possible
