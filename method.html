Method

How this project was organized to compare music conditions fairly.

Participants and Conditions

The tasks were completed under different music conditions.

  • Silence (no music)
  • Instrumental (no lyrics)
  • Lyrical (songs with words)

Tasks and Measurements

  • Reading comprehension
  • Writing or short response
  • Math practice
  • Memorization
  • Accuracy: correct answers or score
  • Time: completion time
  • Self-rating: focus level from 1-10

Controls for Fair Testing

  • Use similar task difficulty for each condition.
  • Keep time limits consistent.
  • Keep volume consistent or recorded.
  • Use the same environment when possible.
