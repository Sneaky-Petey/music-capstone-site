Does music improve concentration while studying?

This capstone project explores how different types of music (silence, instrumental, lyrical) can affect focus and performance during study tasks.

See Results Research Question

Project Snapshot

  • ✅ Compared study conditions (none / instrumental / lyrical)
  • ✅ Looked at task performance + focus ratings
  • ✅ Summarized method, results, and conclusion
  • ✅ Includes an interactive results tool

Research Question

Main question: How does listening to music while studying affect concentration and performance?

Sub-questions:

  • Does instrumental music improve focus compared to silence?
  • Do songs with lyrics increase distraction for reading/writing tasks?
  • Does the effect change depending on the type of task (math vs reading)?

Hypothesis

Prediction

I predicted that instrumental music at moderate volume would improve focus for many students, while lyrical music would reduce performance on reading and writing tasks.

Why this matters

Students commonly study with music. Understanding what helps (or hurts) focus can improve study habits and outcomes.

Key Terms

Concentration

Staying focused on a task without drifting attention.

Performance

How well the task is completed (accuracy, speed, completion).

Distraction

Anything that interrupts attention (lyrics, notifications, noise).
